About HTG

An Australian based cyber security company specializing in offensive security solutions. We offer services to identify, validate and remediate security issues concerning infrastructure and application environments to physical and human assets. Our primary goal is to find the vulnerabilities before they are known to an adversary thus providing an additional layer of preservation towards your information assets.
Our team consists of various talented skillsets founded to assist our clients in the identification, isolation and mitigation of vulnerabilities. We believe that to be defensive, you must know the offensive.
